## Portfolio Metrics

- Target: 10 apps over 18 months
- Combined revenue target: $60K-$240K/year from Store alone
- Store economics: $5,000/year Build Partner fee + 20% revenue share
- Paid apps include embedded custom table entitlements (buyer friction reduction)
- Certification timeline: 3-5 weeks per app

## Build Priority Factors

When prioritizing, weigh these factors:
1. **Market demand evidence** (signals from Vigil)
2. **Build effort vs. revenue ratio** (low effort + high revenue = build first)
3. **NowForge capability readiness** (can NowForge build this today?)
4. **Competitive urgency** (is someone else about to ship this?)
5. **Portfolio diversity** (mix GRC + platform utility for broader reach)
6. **Proof-of-concept value** (does this app validate NowForge to prospects?)
